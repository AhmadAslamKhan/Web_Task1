Top 5 anime you should be watching

Ahmad Aslam
Last updated Sep 10, 2022

5.
Sword Art Online

Click to Watch Now!!

The story revolves around a young teenage boy named Kirito who gets trapped into a new vr game along with 10,000 other people. the catch? If you die in the game you die in real life! and the only escape is by completing the game. Sword Art Online shows kirito's journey, hardships and how his relations build with the people he meets for the first time. the only thing on his mind is survival and trying to complete the game and help everyone escape safely.

Do you like something packed with action, fantasy, romanace or just are fascinated by the concept of next level VR. If so this anime is right up your alley. It has all of these and more. Altough the first arc of the anime is phenomenal and makes it one of my all time favorite. The only downside of the anime is the following arcs are not up to power with the first one.

Genre: Adventure, Science Fiction

4.
Haikyu!!

Click to Watch Now!!

The series revolves around a highschool volleyball team named Karasuno who reached the national once, but had fallen since. Now known as the "Fallen Crows" or the "Flightless Crows" they have lost their place they once had. But things start to change when new first year students join the volleyball club and try to start things to move. Will Karasuno rise to their previous glory? or will they remain forgotten theres only one way to find out.

Haikyu!! is all about sports, light hearted comedy and a healthy rivalry. Haikyu!! is my favorite sports anime of all time. The animation and character development are all top tier. Haikyuu has a way of showing character backstories in a way that youll go through their journey. The anime shows you scenes that will give you goosebumps through out the show. Overall its a must watch anime.

Genre: Sports, Comedy

3.
Jujutsu no kaisen

Click to Watch Now!!

What do you do when you find the cursed finger of the strongest demon to ever exist? well for Yuuji Itadori, he eats it to save his friends making the demon take control of his body. Yuuji is taken by a special group of people that fight these cursed demons and given 2 options. Either hell be killed or he will eat the remaining fingers till he can and then be killed. Yuuji listening to his grandfathers dying request to help others, takes on the mission of fighting demons.

Demons, curses, Supernatural beings, people with extraodinary abilities and a talking fighting panda. If thats not enough to make you watch the show than what is?

Genre: Adventure, Dark fantasy, Supernatural

2.
Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Click to Watch Now!!

Tanjiro Kamado is a young boy who lives in a forest a bit far from a village with his mother and siblings. Being the eldest he cuts and sell wood in the village to make a living for his family. One day he gets late selling the wood and an old man lets him stay for the night telling that its dangerous to go out in the dark. The next day when he returns he finds out his whole family had been killed and his little sisters turned into a blood thirsty demon. can it get any worse? yes! a demon slayer reaches the site. Tanjiro convinces him to help him return his sister to normal and takes up the task of finding and killing the man who did this to his family.

The anime is filled with beautiful art style. Its a well built series with outstanding fight scenes and excelent plot line. overall its a must watch for everyone.

Genre: Adventure, Dark fantasy, Martial arts

1.
Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion

Click to Watch Now!!

The holy empire of Britannia conqures japan. Lelouch an exiled prince after the encounter with a mysterious women who gives him the power to control people through eye contact vows to go against the empire of Britannia which controls most of the world.

If your looking for some old animation style with a good plot that gets you hooked. This is your show. Intelligent main lead, strategy based wars, large scale wars and to top it off robots to control and fight. This show is a classic with everything one would want.

Genre: Action, Drama, Science Fiction
